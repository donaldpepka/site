Projects

  • Decoding A Sign in Space

    A Margolus neighborhood Block Cellular Automaton that replicates the decrypted message from A Sign in Space, a collaborative art project that simulates an extraterrestrial transmission.

  • For the Love of Greed

    An application of LLMs to early modern English texts, this project generated a word embedding from a corpus of several thousand documents and sought to understand shifting notions of consumption through changes in semantic meaning. A collaboration with Albert Sun, Daisy Zhan, and Andrew Scofield; led by Dr. Astrid Giugni and Dr. Jessica Hines.
